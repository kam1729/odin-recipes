TEA

Tea is a very famous indian drink also famous in indian sub continent, having enjoyed by peoples of all the age group mostly in the morning, it act as a starter for a day for most people, it is also taken as a refreshning drink.

Following are the steps written for making tea.

ingredients:

  • Milk
  • Water
  • Tea powder
  • Sugar
  • Ginger
  • Elaichi
  1. Take a cup tea(around 100ml) and put it on boil for some time
  2. After getting water boiled, add some tea powder in it(1 tea spoon/serving) and keep it boiling for some more time
  3. Then add some ginger and elaichi for the flavor and taste
  4. When a tea powder leaves it color into the water, then add some sugar in it(1-2 tea spoon/serving, as per individual taste) keep it stiring for while
  5. Then finally add milk in it(1 cup/serving) and again keep it boiling for a while
  6. After getting boiled, serve it in cups with some snacks as per taste(for example: biscuits).
