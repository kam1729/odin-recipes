DAL

Dal(chana) is very nutrious food in indian sub continent, mostly taken with rice and roti(chapati), it is one of the best source of protein for people living here.

So following are the steps written for making dal.

ingredients:

  • Mustard oil
  • Dal(chana)
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Turmeric powder
  • Red chilli powder
  1. Take some dal(100ml) wash it with water, 2-3 times and then leave it in cup full of water for some time(15 minutes)
  2. Meanwhile cut 2-3 onion and 1-2 tomato in small slices
  3. After that put cooker on flame(low) and put some oil in it, and then put the cut onion and tomato in it and stir it for some time, till onion changes it color to reddish or brownish
  4. After that add the soaked dal in it, add again stir it for some time
  5. Then add some water(3-4 times of dal taken) and close the cooker till 4-5 whistle, now the dal is ready to serve.
