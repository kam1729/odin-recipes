KHEER

Kheer is a very common dish in india and its subcontinent, and it is of many kind like kheer of rice, savaiya, sabudana, carrot and many more

Today we are gonna learn rice kheer recipe.

ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Dry fruits(almonds, kaju, kismis, kesar)
  1. Take some milk(500ml), put it on flame and keep it boiling for some time
  2. In the meantime take some rice(50gm) around one tenth of milk taken and wash it 2-3 times and leave it in water for some time
  3. After boiling add some sugar in it, as per taste, keep it boiling for some more time
  4. After that add the soaked rice in it, again keep boiling for some more time
  5. At last add some dry fruits-almonds, kaju, kismis, and kesar, after 1-2 minutes, its ready to serve.
